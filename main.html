// 01 — Profile

How I actually work.

I'd rather implement Naive Bayes by hand and get the math wrong twice than import a library and never find out how it thinks. That's mostly why my code looks the way it does — from ESP32 firmware to React animation, I try to own the layer I'm building on, not just call it.

Most of what I ship comes out of hackathons and self-directed builds: a hardware-backed agri platform, a role-gated HR system, a handful of NLP experiments, and a running series of ML algorithms rebuilt from scratch and written up for anyone else who wants to see the internals too.

DATA→ MODEL→ DECISION
